Added a page on [redundant entry](/spec/accessibility/redundant-entry/), WCAG 2.2 Success Criterion 3.3.7 (Level A): information a user already entered earlier in the same process must be auto-populated or selectable, not typed again. It covers the essential, security, and expired exceptions and the carry-forward and `autocomplete` patterns that satisfy it.
