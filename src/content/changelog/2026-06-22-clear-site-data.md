Added a page on [`Clear-Site-Data`](/spec/security/clear-site-data/) — the response header that tells the browser to wipe cookies, storage, and caches for your origin. It pairs with [cookie attributes](/spec/security/cookie-attributes/): set the attributes right while a session lives, then clear everything in one header on sign-out.
