Added a page on [Agentic Resource Discovery (ARD)](/spec/agent-readiness/agentic-resource-discovery/), the new Linux Foundation / Google draft that lets a domain publish an AI Catalog at `/.well-known/ai-catalog.json` listing the agent capabilities it offers. The site now ships it as a worked example: our catalog lists the MCP server and A2A agent, advertised through a `Link` header, a `link rel`, a `robots.txt` `Agentmap:` directive, and DNS.
