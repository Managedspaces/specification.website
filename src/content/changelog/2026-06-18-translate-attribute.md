Added a page on [the `translate` attribute](/spec/i18n/translate-attribute/), which marks content that automatic translation systems must leave alone — brand names, code, and identifiers. It complements [the `lang` attribute](/spec/i18n/lang-attribute/): `lang` declares what language content is in, while `translate="no"` keeps Google Translate and the browser's built-in translation from mangling terms that have no localised form.
