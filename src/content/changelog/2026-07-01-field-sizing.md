Added a page on content-based field sizing — the CSS `field-sizing: content` property that lets text inputs, textareas, and selects shrink-wrap and grow with their content, replacing the JavaScript auto-grow hack with one declaration. Documented as `optional`: it reached Baseline in June 2026 (Firefox 152 completing the set) but only applies where a site has form controls, and it degrades safely to the fixed default elsewhere.
