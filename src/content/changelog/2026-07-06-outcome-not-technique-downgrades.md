Downgraded [View Transitions](/spec/performance/view-transitions/), [invoker commands](/spec/foundations/invoker-commands/), and [visibility-aware rendering](/spec/performance/visibility-aware-rendering/) from `recommended` to `optional`. All three are worthwhile platform features, but their benefit is context-dependent polish or applies only to a subset of sites — the bar for `recommended` is that a modern site should do it, and none of these clears it.
